Omega & Epsilon dual PC build

This is my custom built dual pc. The top system is named Epsilon and the bottom system is named Omega. I named them this in reference to a series called Red vs Blue where the AI Epsilon was capable of operating multiple functions at the same time. While Omega was orientated for combat. Hence why Epsilon is a bigger system running the cooling, OBS stream video encoding, spotify, music production, and discord chat. While Omega is primarily focused on gaming, video editing, and coding.
